Welcome To Eben Ezar Restaurant

Little Story

Eben Ezar Company (EEC) is a restaurant led by people passionate about great food. Our team is on a mission to provide the best in class in everything we do serving the best Japanese and other Asian cuisine without skimping on quality, presentation, and cleanliness.

History

The very first Eben Ezar Restaurant opened on March 23, 2022. Located at Abu Bakr Alrazi St, As Sulimaniyah,Riyadh.

Mission

We aim to make Japanese and other Asian food accessible to everyone.
